Cornell and Ithaca Rapid Transit

CIRP is a light rail system that allows people in Ithaca to access a fast and sustainable method of transportation. CIRP crosses multiple regions of Ithaca, pinpointing key locations where people need to access.

The reformation of Ithaca NY's current transit system aims to revolutionize transportation in the area, focusing on sustainability, speed, reliability, and passenger comfort. This project will bring about significant improvements to the existing transit infrastructure.

Our Services

Here at Cornell and Ithaca Rapid Transit, we offer a range of services to meet the diverse needs of our passengers:

  • Light rail transportation across Ithaca
  • Convenient access to key locations
  • Comfortable and efficient travel

About Us

Cornell and Ithaca Rapid Transit is committed to providing sustainable and reliable transportation solutions to the residents and visitors of Ithaca. Our team is dedicated to ensuring the success of this project and the satisfaction of our passengers.

Reformation of Ithaca NY's Current Transit System

We are proud to introduce the reformation of Ithaca NY's current transit system, aimed at making transportation more sustainable, faster, and more reliable, while also providing upgraded comfort and onboard technology.

The Trains Will:

  • Be fully electric
  • Utilize energy from a nearby solar farm
  • Be equipped with charging stations in each car
  • Have heating and AC systems
  • Offer comfortable seating with desk options
  • Feature modular designs for easy replacement
  • Be easily accessible, fast, and autonomous
  • Have multiple emergency exits

This Project:

  • Will improve the lives of locals and visitors
  • Will significantly reduce emissions
  • Will provide reliable transportation for lower-income families
  • Will benefit the economy
  • Will not have a significant ecological impact
  • Could make neighborhoods quieter
  • Will try to utilize already existing infrastructure
  • Will run indefinitely and be maintained by certified technicians
